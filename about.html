About Frank the dog sitter

I've worked in animal hospitals all through high school and have been dog sitting and dog walking various roommates' dogs throughout college.Now that I have graduated, I've got a 9-5 desk job that I enjoy, but also student loans that I don't enjoy.

Let me give you peace of mind when you are away that your dog(s) are in good care. It will reduce your stress and let you travel worry free.

I'm dependable and know what to do should an issue arise. I have reliable transportation and local references upon request.
