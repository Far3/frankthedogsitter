What are your rates?
My rates vary depending on the length of stay, if its a holiday, the amount of dogs under my care and the distance to work if my stay lands during weekdays. Please contact me for a pricing quote.
What is the process for prospective clients?
You can contact me any time of the day via text or e-mail with the porposed dates, location and any other information. I will schedule a meet and greet beforehand at no charge. This will allow me to meet everyone at your house and answer any questions that you may have.

If everything works out we will arange dates and times and I will send over a template for you to fill out with everything I need during my stay.

When I arrive I will contact you and keep you updated throughout my stay. On my final day I will clean up and leave the place better than I found it when you return.

Do you have any deals or specials for current clients?
I have a promotion going on right now for 2 free nights, if you refer me to another client and we have a meet and greet.
You're always booked! Do you have any other local dog sitters that you could reccommend?
I do have several contacts that I have personally vetted that could cover for me if I happen to be double booked. Please contact me for details.
